# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
Business owners, founders, and marketing teams who want a partner, not a flashy vendor. Primary audience is B2B clients looking for growth, branding, and custom software solutions.

## Product Purpose
A 2026 growth studio marketing site to acquire B2B clients, showcase case studies, and offer AI solutions. It aims to position Indiefluence as a serious, psychology-led partner.

## Positioning
Psychology-led growth studio. From ideas to impact. We build brands, campaigns, content and software that move real people to act.

## Conversion & proof
- Primary and secondary CTA: Primary CTA is "Book a meeting". Secondary fallbacks are WhatsApp/phone, email, and a short lead form.
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: A psychology-led growth studio that moves real people to act.
- Belief ladder: Visitors must believe that Indiefluence understands human psychology, delivers measurable business impact, and has the track record to prove it before booking a meeting.
- Proof on hand: 80+ brands, 7 countries served, in-house studio, Times of India feature, and real client case studies.

## Brand Personality
Confident, specific, business-friendly, serious, and honest.

## Anti-references
Scam websites, generic agency templates, casino pages, loud ad-tech pages, crypto funnels, or AI gimmick landing pages.

## Design Principles
1. **Show, don't just tell:** Use real client/work visuals and concrete case studies over vague claims.
2. **Honesty over hype:** Be plainspoken and specific; avoid buzzwords like "revolutionary" or "10x".
3. **Mobile-first clarity:** Ensure every page is excellent at 360px width with one major thought per viewport.
4. **Confident restraint:** Maximal in content and confidence, minimal in composition (no heavy shadows or endless glassmorphism).
5. **Intentional motion:** Use motion to make the experience feel alive, but never at the expense of performance or readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard web accessibility (WCAG). Respect reduced-motion preferences for animations. Ensure high readability with clear typography and adequate contrast.

Print N Pour is a D2C brand making 3D-printed coffee accessories for people who take their pour-over seriously. It needed a storefront with the craft and personality of the products themselves.

## The challenge

D2C is crowded, and coffee especially so. The site had to feel considered and tactile, and convert, without the templated look that makes a small brand blend into every other small brand.

## Our thinking

We designed around the ritual of coffee: unhurried, precise, a little obsessive in the best way. The build prioritised a smooth path from browsing to basket, because a beautiful shop that's hard to buy from is just a gallery.

## The work

We built a fully responsive storefront with cart, quick-view, category filtering and saved-basket persistence, all in a self-contained build, styled to match the brand's V60-loving, detail-first personality.

## Where it landed

A storefront that looks like the products it sells: crafted, deliberate and made for people who care about the details.

Genesis Classes, a coaching institution, needed to fill its Dropper Batch for NEET and JEE aspirants — a decision families make carefully, on trust as much as anything else.

## The challenge

Education marketing lives or dies on credibility. Push too hard and you lose parents; too soft and you're invisible. The message had to be factual and reassuring, while still moving families to act before enrolment closed.

## Our thinking

We built a factual-forward tone with a soft close, letting results and reputation do the persuading. Creative was designed for how the audience actually consumes — short reels, local voices, the institution's own credibility front and centre.

## The work

We wrote Hinglish video ad scripts tuned to the audience, recommended reel formats and audio, and shaped a campaign that put facts first and the hard sell last, with the institution's details where a ready family would look for them.

## Where it landed

A campaign that spoke the audience's language, literally and tonally, and treated a serious family decision with the seriousness it deserves.

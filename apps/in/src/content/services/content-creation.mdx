Welcome to the ‘Mute Box’, where "content" doesn't mean another cringey stock video or blog post written by an
intern with Google open. At Indiefluence, we only roll with the kind of creators who can make your brand look
good, sound smart, and - let's be honest - shut up your competition.

We shoot videos that make people forget the skip button exists. Our podcasts? Actual humans listen (and not
just for the guest's LinkedIn plug). Blogs? We don't just write them for SEO robots - we write them for real
people who, you know, actually read stuff.

We believe good stories sell, bad stories bore, and average stories… well, let's just say you won't find those
here. So if you want content that's more "wow, who made that?" and less "ugh, not another listicle," you know
where to find us.

# A New Chapter

We're excited to announce the launch of our completely redesigned website, built from the ground up to reflect our psychology-led approach to business growth and engineering.

## What's changed?

- **Blazing Fast Performance**: We migrated from Next.js to Astro to deliver a static, lightning-fast experience with zero client-side JavaScript overhead where it's not needed.
- **Upgraded Visuals**: We've integrated fluid typography, smooth GSAP animations, and a cohesive design system based on our core brand values.
- **Refined Copy**: Every word has been crafted to ensure we're communicating our mission clearly: turning ideas into impact.
